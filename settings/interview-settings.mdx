## Modifying Settings for a Specific Interview

Settings for a specific interview may be modified by navigating to the job, selecting the **Configure** tab and then selecting the **Settings** tab on configuration sidebar.


<Frame caption="Job > Configure > Settings">
<img src="/images/job-settings-page.png" alt="Calendar integration setup screen" />
</Frame>


From there, you can configure settings such as
- **Outreach Settings** The number and frequency of outreach attempts as well as permitted outreach hours
- **Results Settings** Who should receive email updates about the search results, whether to collect candidate feedback, and how to customize the interview summary
- **Caller Persona** Customize the voice, name, and personality of your interviewer




along with many other configuration options. The full settings configuration overview is outlined in [Available Settings](/settings/available-settings)
